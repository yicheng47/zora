Store a durable, curated memory. Unlike observations (raw signals), memories are interpreted understanding.

Use this for:
- identity: who the user is — background, role, how they see themselves
- disposition: how they operate — the tensions and contradictions, not averages
- context: what they're working on and WHY — motivations matter more than tasks

A disposition memory should capture contradictions explicitly: "meticulous about X but careless about Y" is more valuable than "generally careful."
